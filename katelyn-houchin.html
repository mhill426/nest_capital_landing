Katelyn Houchin

Executive Assistant

Katelyn serves as the Executive Assistant for our Chief of Operations Officer. She came to us with many years of experience in the administrative field. Her attention-to-detail and the ability to make sure the job gets done is what makes her the best assistant ever! She handles a variety of tasks within the daily operations for each location within the practice including compliance needs, client outreach, and special projects. You may also catch her assisting clients or answering the phone at the Bellefontaine office from time-to-time.

Katelyn has been native to Logan County her whole life. She enjoys attending live concerts, playing competitive softball and outdoor activities. She is usually very busy entertaining her three young children, Raelyn, Jordyn, and Jaxtyn as well as her Great Dane, Jazzy.
