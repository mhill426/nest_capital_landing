About

Our Story

Katterhenry Investment Group was founded in 1989, and is as strong as ever with the ability to offer you seven advisors “under one roof”.

All seven of our advisors are fully equipped with Series 7 and 63/65 securities registrations and life insurance licenses. We also have two CFP® professionals, a Registered Principal, two Chartered Retirement Planning CounselorSM, a Chartered Mutual Fund CounselorsSM, and a Master of Science Degree in Financial Planning within our ranks. It is rare, indeed, to find over a hundred years of combined industry experience and that high level of certification and education within one team. We help clients manage more than $738 million in assets, as of January 31, 2022, with five locations for your convenience. Each of us brings a distinct set of experience and skills to the mix, coming together complimentarily to create an incredibly effective team.

Retirement planning is the heart of our business. We work with multiple generations to facilitate wealth accumulation, retirement income, wealth preservation, and wealth transfer. With unwavering disciplined commitment to our core investment management practices and beliefs, we endeavor to grow client assets during favorable market conditions, as well as preserve their wealth during rough times in the financial markets.

Services


  • Investment Management: Determining your asset-allocation needs and true diversification. Helping you understand your risk tolerance. Recommending the appropriate investment vehicles to help you reach your goals.
  • Risk Management: Reviewing existing life, disability, and long-term care insurance policies. Help finding a new suitable policy for your specific situation.
  • Employee and Executive Benefits: Helping your business attract and retain qualified employees through benefit packages.
  • Tax Management: Making sure your portfolio is tax-efficient. Developing tax-efficient investment strategies. Referring you to qualified tax specialists.
  • Estate Planning and Wealth Transfer Strategies: Help review your wills and trusts, preserve your estate for your intended heirs, establish beneficiary designations, reduce potential exposure to estate taxes and probate costs, and coordinate with your tax and legal advisors.
  • Education Funding: Recommending investment and accumulation strategies to help you pay for your children's and grandchildren's educational pursuits.
