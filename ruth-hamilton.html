Ruth Hamilton

Training and Development Coordinator

Ruth is a long-time friend of Katterhenry Investment Group and joins us with 19 years of experience in Financial Services. As our Training and Development Coordinator in the Dublin office, she provides excellent service to our team and the individuals who place their trust in us. Ruth also works closely with Tim to help support the NEST Capital brand.

Ruth believes in giving back to her community and actively volunteers with Ohio Health Hospice. She and her husband Keith reside in Dublin and enjoy spending their time outdoors, eating spicy food in New Mexico and listening to live music. Together they have six grown children, several grandkids and two big crazy dogs.
