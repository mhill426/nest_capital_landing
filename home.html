Our Story

NEST Capital was founded with a focused mission: to help financial advisors find a better path to independence: to take back control of the client experience and to provide more optionality to their own business, continuity, or succession plans. Why Nest

For you.
For your clients.
For your legacy.

For decades advisors in the traditional employee-model firms have had limited choice in platforms for delivering unbiased, unfiltered advice, planning, and solutions to clients. Wirehouse brokerage firm options were even more limited, especially if you were located outside of a major metro area. Besides that, financial advisors were made to believe going independent was a bridge too far…that you would spend all your time hiring, firing, and paying utility bills instead of actually working with clients. When you got into this business, you were likely told that this was your business and that you were running your own practice. But the reality is that most broker/dealers look at your clients as their own; that clients work with an advisor because of the company on the business card. And how about compensation plans? Are they adjusted each year leaving you constantly pivoting?

Nest Capital is built for successful, experienced advisors who know what they want for their practice and who know how to delegate tasks that get in the way of focusing on clients. NEST Capital…for you….for your clients….for your legacy.


Your Goals
  • Succession Track
  • Growth Track
  • Continuity Plan

What happens when you retire? Your clients get redistributed by the branch to the favorite advisors. Why not take control of that process and choose your own successor: one you trust to take as good a care of your clients as you have. No one knows your clients better than you. And they trust your judgement and recommendations. Choose a successor that you TRUST.

Your career is about to takeoff. Your runway is still long. Retirement is over the horizon and you have big plans for your practice, your associates, and your clients. Finding a trusted partner who can take care of administrative tasks frees you up to focus on even greater growth.

NEST Capital can serve as your continuity partner buying you time until your successor is ready to take over the business.

Affiliation Models
  • Full Affiliation
  • Affiliation Lite

For those who like and appreciate the balance of responsibilities with your current home office but want more flexibility while operating and advising clients on an independent platform. You understand that what you enjoy most about this business is working with people. And you want to delegate those time-consuming functions like compliance, operations, marketing, and research to our highly experienced team. Unlike before, this truly is Your Business, Your Process. Your Advice…..to do what is in the best interest of YOUR clients. Affiliation is the model most similar to your current situation but with all the benefits of being independent.

For the most entrepreneurial-minded advisors. You want to hang your own shingle, but you still value a few outsourced functions in operations or compliance, for example. You feel the call to not only work IN the business but ON the business too. But you also know when to delegate responsibilities and might wish to take management, compliance, and/or HR functions of your plate. We have a tailored solution for this scenario as well. Affiliation Lite is a Supported-DIY solution operating more hands-on and more autonomously on the business.

How do I choose?

The great news is that regardless of how you affiliate, you will have realized the dream of working for yourself….and in the best interests of your clients. Unbiased Advice…..Customized Service….An Accretive Business….all while successfully running your practice the same way you always have by utilizing all the same resources to which you and your clients have grown accustomed.
